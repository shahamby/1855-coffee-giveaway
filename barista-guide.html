Barista Guide
What is this?
A customer donated 30 days of free coffee to give away right here at 1855. The winner each day is chosen by a real mathematical phenomenon called the Birthday Paradox — the same math used in cybersecurity to explain how hackers can crack passwords much faster than most people expect.

All you do is ask each loyalty customer one question and tap their answer into the app. The app does the rest — it watches for a match and tells you exactly when a winner appears.
How to run it each day
  1. 1
    Open the app on the tablet or phone at the start of your shift.
    URL is at the bottom of this guide. Bookmark it — it remembers where you left off.
  2. 2
    For each loyalty customer, ask them kindly:
    "Hey, could I ask your birthday? Just the month and day — no year needed." Most people enjoy it. It feels like a fun little lottery.
  3. 3
    Tap their month on the grid, then tap their day.
    The app moves to the day screen automatically after you pick the month.
  4. 4
    Watch the collision probability bar climb with each customer.
    Once it crosses 50%, a match could happen any moment.
  5. 5
    When a winner triggers, the app shows a big winner screen automatically — no guessing involved.
    Award that customer one free coffee. Tap "Coffee awarded" to return to the daily view.
  6. 6
    At the end of the day, tap Barista Controls → Start Day [X] to advance to tomorrow.
    If no winner was found, use "Skip today — no winner" instead.
Good to know
Only loyalty customers count. You don't need to ask every person in line — just customers who present a loyalty card or account when ordering.
No personal information is stored. The app only remembers that a birthday was entered — never the customer's name or anything else. It resets its list every day.
Months with a small amber dot already have at least one entry for the day. This is a visual hint — it does not affect the outcome. Every birthday still counts equally.
The app works offline. Once it has loaded on the device, you don't need Wi-Fi to use it. Just don't clear the browser data or it will forget the current day's entries.
Quick answers
What if a customer doesn't want to share their birthday?
No problem at all — just skip them and move to the next customer. Participation is optional and should feel fun, not pressured.
What if I enter a birthday incorrectly?
Tap the month button on the second screen that says "← change" to go back and start over for that customer. Entries already submitted cannot be removed, so just re-enter the correct one for the same customer as the next entry.
What does the "Birthday Paradox" actually mean?
In a group of just 23 people, there is a greater than 50% chance that two of them share the same birthday. Most people guess you'd need hundreds. The math surprises almost everyone — which is exactly why it's used in cybersecurity to explain how hackers find collisions in password systems faster than expected. The cards Shannon left explain it further.
A winner was found but I wasn't ready — can I undo it?
The winner cannot be undone once triggered. Award the free coffee as normal and tap "Coffee awarded" to continue the day. The day is marked as complete after a winner is found — no further entries are accepted until tomorrow.
The app looks wrong or isn't loading correctly.
Do a hard refresh: hold Shift and press F5 on a keyboard, or close and reopen the browser tab. If the issue continues, email Shannon at shambu12sudo62117@proton.me.
